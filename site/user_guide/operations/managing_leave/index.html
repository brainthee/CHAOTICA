    Managing Leave¶

    CHAOTICA includes a leave management system to help track team availability and ensure proper resource planning. This guide covers requesting, approving, and managing leave requests.

    Internal Use Only

    The leave tracking system is for internal convenience only and is not a replacement for corporate HR functions. Always follow your organization's official HR policies and procedures.

    Leave Types¶

    Standard Leave Types¶

    Annual Leave - Vacation time and planned time off - Requires advance notice (configurable, default 14 days) - Subject to approval by manager - Counts against annual leave balance

    Sick Leave - Unplanned absence due to illness - Can be requested retrospectively - Usually requires minimal approval process - May require medical documentation for extended periods

    Personal Leave - Personal time off for family or personal matters - Planned absence for personal reasons - Requires manager approval - May count against leave balance

    Training Leave - Time off for professional development - Conference attendance and training courses - Often encouraged and approved readily - May be funded by organization

    Maternity/Paternity Leave - Extended leave for new parents - Longer duration (weeks/months) - Requires advance planning - Follows legal and company policies

    Emergency Leave - Unplanned urgent personal matters - Family emergencies or critical situations - Flexible approval process - Case-by-case consideration

    Custom Leave Types¶

    Organizations can configure additional leave types: - Bereavement leave - Jury duty - Military service - Study leave - Sabbatical leave - Unpaid leave

    Requesting Leave¶

    Prerequisites¶

    • Active user account with appropriate permissions
    • Understanding of your leave balance and entitlements
    • Awareness of team workload and commitments
    • Knowledge of advance notice requirements

    Step-by-Step Process¶

    1. Navigate to Leave Management
    2. Go to your user dashboard
    3. Click "Leave" in the navigation menu

    4. Click "Request Leave"

    5. Select Leave Type 

      Leave Type: Select appropriate type from dropdown
Category: Planned/Unplanned
Priority: Normal/Urgent

    6. Define Leave Period 

      Start Date: First day of leave
End Date: Last day of leave (inclusive)
Partial Days: Half days or specific hours (if supported)
Working Days Only: Exclude weekends and holidays

    7. Provide Details 

      Reason: Brief explanation for the leave
Comments: Additional context or information
Contact Information: How to reach you if needed
Emergency Contact: Alternative contact if urgent

    8. Coverage Planning 

      Coverage Arrangements: Who will handle your work
Handover Notes: Key information for coverage person
Critical Dates: Important deadlines during your absence
Client Communication: How clients should be handled

    9. Submit Request

    10. Review all information for accuracy
    11. Submit request to your manager
    12. Monitor request status in the system
    13. Follow up if needed

    Leave Request Guidelines¶

    Advance Notice: - Annual leave: Default 14 days notice (configurable) - Training: As much notice as possible - Personal leave: Reasonable notice based on circumstances - Emergency leave: Submit as soon as possible

    Duration Considerations: - Short leave (1-3 days): Minimal impact planning - Medium leave (1-2 weeks): Detailed coverage arrangements - Extended leave (>2 weeks): Comprehensive handover planning - Recurring leave: Consider impact on team and clients

    Approval Process¶

    Approval Hierarchy¶

    Direct Manager: - Primary approval for most leave requests - Considers team workload and coverage - Reviews business impact - Approves within policy guidelines

    Department Manager: - Extended leave approval (>2 weeks) - Cross-team impact considerations - Resource planning coordination - Policy exception decisions

    HR Approval: - Leave policy compliance - Legal requirement verification - Extended absence coordination - Return-to-work planning

    Approval Criteria¶

    Factors Considered: - Team workload and availability - Client commitments and deadlines - Coverage arrangements adequacy - Leave balance and entitlements - Business impact assessment - Policy compliance

    Automatic Approvals: - Sick leave (with limits) - Emergency leave (retrospective approval) - Training leave (pre-approved categories) - Standard annual leave (within guidelines)

    Manager Review Required: - Extended leave periods - Peak business periods - Insufficient coverage - Multiple team members requesting same period - Policy exceptions

    Approval Timeline¶

    Standard Processing: - Routine requests: 2-3 business days - Extended leave: 5-10 business days - Emergency requests: Same day (retrospective) - Complex requests: Up to 2 weeks

    Factors Affecting Timeline: - Manager availability - Coverage complexity - Client impact assessment - Policy compliance review - Resource planning requirements

    Managing Leave Balance¶

    Leave Entitlements¶

    Annual Entitlement: - Based on employment contract - Pro-rated for part-time employees - Adjusted for start date during year - May increase with tenure

    Carry Forward: - Unused leave may carry forward - Usually limited amount (e.g., 5-10 days) - Use-it-or-lose-it policies may apply - Dependent on organization policy

    Leave Accrual: - Monthly or pay-period accrual - Based on hours worked - Pro-rated for part-time staff - Capped at maximum balance

    Balance Monitoring¶

    Current Balance: - Available leave days remaining - Pending requests impact - Future entitlements - Projected year-end balance

    Usage Tracking: - Leave taken year-to-date - Average monthly usage - Seasonal patterns - Comparison with previous years

    Planning Tools: - Leave planner calendar - Balance projection tools - Team leave overview - Conflict identification

    Manager Functions¶

    Reviewing Requests¶

    Request Assessment: - Team availability and workload - Client impact and commitments - Coverage adequacy - Business continuity

    Approval Workflow: 1. Receive notification of request 2. Review request details and context 3. Assess team impact and coverage 4. Consult with stakeholders if needed 5. Approve, decline, or request modifications 6. Communicate decision to requestor

    Team Leave Management¶

    Leave Calendar: - Visual overview of team leave - Identify potential conflicts - Plan for peak absence periods - Coordinate coverage arrangements

    Resource Planning: - Project timeline adjustments - Client communication planning - Temporary resource arrangements - Cross-training initiatives

    Policy Enforcement: - Ensure compliance with leave policies - Handle policy exceptions appropriately - Document decisions and rationale - Maintain fairness and consistency

    Leave Analytics¶

    Team Metrics: - Leave utilization rates - Average leave duration - Seasonal patterns - Approval/denial rates

    Planning Insights: - Identify peak leave periods - Plan resource requirements - Assess policy effectiveness - Support workforce planning

    Notifications and Alerts¶

    Manager Alerts: - New requests requiring approval - Overdue approval notifications - Team leave conflicts - Policy compliance issues

    Team Notifications: - Colleague leave announcements - Coverage arrangement updates - Schedule change notifications - Return date confirmations

    Personal Calendars: - Sync with Outlook/Google Calendar - Leave periods marked as busy - Automatic meeting declines - Out-of-office message triggers

    Team Calendars: - Shared team leave calendar - Resource availability overview - Project planning integration - Client-facing calendar updates

    Reporting and Analytics¶

    Individual Reports: - Leave balance statements - Usage history and patterns - Pending request summaries - Future entitlement projections

    Management Reports: - Team leave summaries - Department-wide analytics - Policy compliance reports - Resource planning data

    HR Systems¶

    Data Synchronization: - Employee information updates - Leave entitlement calculations - Policy rule enforcement - Payroll system integration

    Compliance Reporting: - Legal requirement compliance - Audit trail maintenance - Policy adherence monitoring - Documentation management

    Project Management¶

    Schedule Impact: - Project timeline adjustments - Resource reallocation - Milestone date updates - Client communication triggers

    Capacity Planning: - Team availability forecasting - Skills gap identification - Resource demand planning - Training need assessment

    Best Practices¶

    For Employees¶

    Planning Ahead: - Plan leave well in advance - Consider team and client impacts - Coordinate with colleagues - Maintain adequate coverage

    Communication: - Provide clear handover instructions - Set up out-of-office messages - Inform clients appropriately - Update project stakeholders

    Flexibility: - Be willing to adjust dates if needed - Consider alternative arrangements - Support team coverage needs - Maintain professional relationships

    For Managers¶

    Fair and Consistent: - Apply policies consistently - Consider individual circumstances - Document decisions clearly - Communicate rationale

    Business Continuity: - Plan for peak leave periods - Develop coverage strategies - Cross-train team members - Maintain client relationships

    Employee Support: - Encourage leave taking - Support work-life balance - Plan around personal needs - Foster team collaboration

    For Organizations¶

    Policy Development: - Clear and comprehensive policies - Regular policy reviews - Legal compliance assurance - Employee feedback incorporation

    System Optimization: - User-friendly interfaces - Efficient approval workflows - Comprehensive reporting - Integration with other systems

    Troubleshooting¶

    Common Issues¶

    Request Delays: - Check manager availability - Verify all required information provided - Follow up on pending approvals - Escalate if necessary

    Balance Discrepancies: - Verify calculation methodology - Check for unrecorded leave - Review policy changes - Contact system administrators

    Coverage Problems: - Identify alternative coverage options - Negotiate date changes - Consider partial leave arrangements - Plan additional handover time

    System Access Issues: - Verify user permissions - Check system availability - Clear browser cache - Contact technical support

    Policy Questions¶

    Leave Entitlements: - Consult employee handbook - Contact HR department - Review legal requirements - Seek manager clarification

    Approval Processes: - Follow established workflows - Understand approval criteria - Document special circumstances - Maintain communication records

    Configuration Settings¶

    Leave Types: - Add custom leave categories - Set approval requirements - Configure balance calculations - Define carry-forward rules

    Notice Periods: - Set minimum notice requirements - Configure automatic approvals - Define escalation procedures - Set manager response times

    Balance Management: - Configure accrual rates - Set maximum balances - Define carry-forward limits - Manage leave year dates

    Integration Settings: - Calendar synchronization - HR system connections - Notification preferences - Reporting parameters
