# Managing Leave

CHAOTICA includes a leave management system to help track team availability and ensure proper resource planning. This guide covers requesting, approving, and managing leave requests.

!!! note "Internal Use Only"
    The leave tracking system is for internal convenience only and is not a replacement for corporate HR functions. Always follow your organization's official HR policies and procedures.

## Leave Types

### Standard Leave Types

**Annual Leave**
- Vacation time and planned time off
- Requires advance notice (configurable, default 14 days)
- Subject to approval by manager
- Counts against annual leave balance

**Sick Leave**
- Unplanned absence due to illness
- Can be requested retrospectively
- Usually requires minimal approval process
- May require medical documentation for extended periods

**Personal Leave**
- Personal time off for family or personal matters
- Planned absence for personal reasons
- Requires manager approval
- May count against leave balance

**Training Leave**
- Time off for professional development
- Conference attendance and training courses
- Often encouraged and approved readily
- May be funded by organization

**Maternity/Paternity Leave**
- Extended leave for new parents
- Longer duration (weeks/months)
- Requires advance planning
- Follows legal and company policies

**Emergency Leave**
- Unplanned urgent personal matters
- Family emergencies or critical situations
- Flexible approval process
- Case-by-case consideration

### Custom Leave Types

Organizations can configure additional leave types:
- Bereavement leave
- Jury duty
- Military service
- Study leave
- Sabbatical leave
- Unpaid leave

## Requesting Leave

### Prerequisites

- Active user account with appropriate permissions
- Understanding of your leave balance and entitlements
- Awareness of team workload and commitments
- Knowledge of advance notice requirements

### Step-by-Step Process

1. **Navigate to Leave Management**
   - Go to your user dashboard
   - Click "Leave" in the navigation menu
   - Click "Request Leave"

2. **Select Leave Type**
   ```
   Leave Type: Select appropriate type from dropdown
   Category: Planned/Unplanned
   Priority: Normal/Urgent
   ```

3. **Define Leave Period**
   ```
   Start Date: First day of leave
   End Date: Last day of leave (inclusive)
   Partial Days: Half days or specific hours (if supported)
   Working Days Only: Exclude weekends and holidays
   ```

4. **Provide Details**
   ```
   Reason: Brief explanation for the leave
   Comments: Additional context or information
   Contact Information: How to reach you if needed
   Emergency Contact: Alternative contact if urgent
   ```

5. **Coverage Planning**
   ```
   Coverage Arrangements: Who will handle your work
   Handover Notes: Key information for coverage person
   Critical Dates: Important deadlines during your absence
   Client Communication: How clients should be handled
   ```

6. **Submit Request**
   - Review all information for accuracy
   - Submit request to your manager
   - Monitor request status in the system
   - Follow up if needed

### Leave Request Guidelines

**Advance Notice**:
- Annual leave: Default 14 days notice (configurable)
- Training: As much notice as possible
- Personal leave: Reasonable notice based on circumstances
- Emergency leave: Submit as soon as possible

**Duration Considerations**:
- Short leave (1-3 days): Minimal impact planning
- Medium leave (1-2 weeks): Detailed coverage arrangements
- Extended leave (>2 weeks): Comprehensive handover planning
- Recurring leave: Consider impact on team and clients

## Approval Process

### Approval Hierarchy

**Direct Manager**:
- Primary approval for most leave requests
- Considers team workload and coverage
- Reviews business impact
- Approves within policy guidelines

**Department Manager**:
- Extended leave approval (>2 weeks)
- Cross-team impact considerations
- Resource planning coordination
- Policy exception decisions

**HR Approval**:
- Leave policy compliance
- Legal requirement verification
- Extended absence coordination
- Return-to-work planning

### Approval Criteria

**Factors Considered**:
- Team workload and availability
- Client commitments and deadlines
- Coverage arrangements adequacy
- Leave balance and entitlements
- Business impact assessment
- Policy compliance

**Automatic Approvals**:
- Sick leave (with limits)
- Emergency leave (retrospective approval)
- Training leave (pre-approved categories)
- Standard annual leave (within guidelines)

**Manager Review Required**:
- Extended leave periods
- Peak business periods
- Insufficient coverage
- Multiple team members requesting same period
- Policy exceptions

### Approval Timeline

**Standard Processing**:
- Routine requests: 2-3 business days
- Extended leave: 5-10 business days
- Emergency requests: Same day (retrospective)
- Complex requests: Up to 2 weeks

**Factors Affecting Timeline**:
- Manager availability
- Coverage complexity
- Client impact assessment
- Policy compliance review
- Resource planning requirements

## Managing Leave Balance

### Leave Entitlements

**Annual Entitlement**:
- Based on employment contract
- Pro-rated for part-time employees
- Adjusted for start date during year
- May increase with tenure

**Carry Forward**:
- Unused leave may carry forward
- Usually limited amount (e.g., 5-10 days)
- Use-it-or-lose-it policies may apply
- Dependent on organization policy

**Leave Accrual**:
- Monthly or pay-period accrual
- Based on hours worked
- Pro-rated for part-time staff
- Capped at maximum balance

### Balance Monitoring
